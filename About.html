About us

Welcome to Ground Break Billiards Cafe a place where you can relax, have fun, and spend real quality time with family and friends. Founded on October 31, 2024 by Froilan Garcia and Shine Basillio, Ground Break was created to be a safe and welcoming space for everyone. We believe that time spent together matters, so we built a café where people can connect through simple moments, laughter, and shared experiences. We’re more than just a billiard café. Whether you’re playing a casual game, singing your heart out in karaoke, or enjoying a meal together, there’s something here for everyone. Our VIP rooms and all-day breakfast make it easy for families, students, and groups to feel comfortable and at home.
